Meet the Founding Barber

Antonio Botticelli

Born in Italy, Antonio began his professional barber career in Rome, where he mastered the art of cutting hair. Five years ago, he moved to the US, bringing his talents and honed skills to a popular barber shop in Washington, D.C.

Antonio is well-versed in a range of hair care practices such as styling, cutting, and trimming. He continues to stay informed about all the latest barbering trends and hair care techniques.

He has a proven record of assessing customers' needs and meeting quality barbering services standards. His passion for his job has transformed cutting hair into an art and translates well to the strong relationship he has formed throughout the barbering industry.
